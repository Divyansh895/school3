Campus Life

Dance Room

  • Dance is a non verbal communication which involves rhythm, expression & social interaction, generally presented in a spiritual or performance setting. The school through its well trained and experienced dance faculty offers training to the students for various dance forms like, disco, tango, hip-hop, ballet and ball room dancing, kathak etc. The dance rooms are beautifully furnished and equipped with latest music systems and recording systems that ensures to maintain a good quality of training imparted to the students.

Music Room

  • In addition to the school curriculum, individual music classes on a variety of instruments are given by a group of qualified music teachers who take Students are encouraged to participate whenever the occasion arises. Participation gives a wonderful opportunity to learn music with their friends, to learn, to play in a group situation and to extend their appreciation of music. They are great motivators and are probably the main reason for the strength of instrumental music at our school.

Auditorium

  • The school auditorium is centrally AC, well equipped with an advance projector, sound and light system. The seating capacity of the Auditorium is around 300, which is sufficient to provide ample opportunities for students to showcase their talents through various activities like Dramatics, Debates, Declamations, Quiz Competitions, Music Competitions. Every month a selected movie is shown to the students of Kindergarten in the Auditorium.

Guidance and Counselling

The guidance and counselling of students is an integral component of the educational mission of the school. Guidance and counselling service and programmes promote the personal/social, educational and career development of the students. We have a well qualified and experienced counsellor for the aid of students and parents. Regular workshops are conducted for parents, students as well as teachers on the various issues of concern and they are encouraged to come up with their individual problems. Modules according to the different age groups are shown to the students on the various topics, followed by brainstorming and detailed discussions. Group therapy as well as individual therapy sessions is being provided to the students as per their requirements. Classroom observations are made as well as regular interaction with the teachers and parents is done so as to streamline the process of guidance and counselling and increase its efficacy.

KLI celebrates the victory of heroic Kargil warriors

Our flag does not fly because the wind moves it. It flies with the last breath of each soldier who died protecting it. To rekindle the pride and valour of the soldiers Kargil Vijay Diwas was observed by K. L. International. A special assembly was held to mark this day wherein a poem competition was organised for kindergarteners to amplify their recitation skills and confidence. To add on to the celebration of this remarkable day, klites expressed their gratitude towards the martyred Indian soldiers through various activities. Beginning with the speech of gratitude then the documentary on Kargil war was presented by the students, followed by showcasing a self-composed book 'Custodians of Nation' and a dance performance on a Kargil Song. The assembly was concluded by the speech of the principal, who made students remember the courageous effort of the soldiers in safeguarding our beautiful country.

An Exhilarating Celebration of PV Sindhu’s Birthday at KLI

“Opportunities don’t happen. You create them.” Rationalizing the statement, PV Sindhu who was born on 5 July 1995 to former volleyball players PV Ramana and PV Vijaya has proved this time and again by winning multitudinous medals at a young age. Sindhu has been an inspiration to many young aspiring players in India, especially girls, who look up to her as a role model. To celebrate the birthday of this sports bigwig, class IV students arranged a notable assembly where they portrayed her journey, her battles, her victories and her never give-up approach towards life to stimulate the students and follow their passion. Students also elucidated the achievements of PV Sindhu through their speech. A hypnotic dance on skates was performed by our proficient students that presented her story of life which was indeed a treat to the eyes. Mr Sudhanshu Shekhar, the School Principal cherished every moment and illuminated the students to get influenced by this rising star and keep a positive and never-quitting attitude like her.

A Blissful Triumph of KLites in JEE ADVANCED

“A dream does not become reality through magic; it takes sweat, determination and hard work.” Our assiduous students burned a candle at both ends to get through JEE ADVANCED examination and their persistent hard work paid off with the result declaration. Apoorv Bhardwaj added a feather in his cap by securing 4829 Rank which proves to be a great achievement in itself whereas Rannvijay Tyagi and Utkarsh Varun also came out with flying colors. The entire school fraternity with the School Principal, Mr Sudhanshu Shekhar, expressed their ecstasy by applauding for the achievers and bestowed their blessings and good wishes on them.

Klites epitomise Yoga on International Yoga days

Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self and what better day to celebrate yourself through Yoga than International Yoga Day. Therefore, to embark on this journey of self-actualisation and let the world know of its importance, the students of our school celebrated International Yoga Day on 21 June. This vital day commenced with our children discussing the significance of Yoga in daily lives and went on further when the students not only enacted the roles of Yoga Gurus but also performed various yoga postures and aasanas. Towards the end of this exemplary day the children became the campaigners for yoga as a way of living and pledged to adopt it in their daily lives.

Glorifying Victory of Klites in Skating Championship

The minor fall, the major lift. Dancing on wheels is always an enchanting experience and what makes it two fold delightful is when you procure medals in it. KLIs demonstrated their skating skills remarkably in Open District Roller Skating Championship held at Meerut. Children who triumphed and brought gold medals for the schools are Dhananjay Soam, Bhoomi Rana, Ameya Jayant, Anshika Verma, Sakshi Tomar. The list does not end here; Rihansh Singh, Jaspreet, Arnav Tiwari, Samridhi Bansal and Leysha Bhutani contributed equally in fetching gold. The crowning accomplishments of the students left everybody spellbound when along with 10 Golds, 04 Silver and 08 Bronze were seized by Klites. The winning streak of the students was benevolently acknowledged by the Principal Mr. Sudhanshu Shekhar which boosted the morale of our high spirited students.
